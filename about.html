About ASTECH

Our Journey Since 2004

PT Aneka Solusi Teknologi (ASTECH) was established in January 2004 and has grown to become a pioneer and leading provider of After-Sales Service in Indonesia. With nationwide coverage, we serve a wide range of products including Consumer Electronics, Computers, Home Appliances, and Mobile Phones.

Through our service brand M-Care, ASTECH operates both Authorized Exclusive Service Centers and Authorized Multi-Brand Service Centers, as well as On-site and Home Service for customers across Indonesia.

With over two decades of experience, we have been a trusted business partner for many global top-brand companies, consistently delivering reliable and high-quality after-sales services to individual consumers (B2C) and corporate clients (B2B).
